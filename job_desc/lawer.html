Lawyer

Job Description

A lawyer is a person that represents other people in the court and their goal is to convince the judges that the client is right and must win the trial.

Workplace

Lawyers work in the court but in order to prepare their defence,they need an office to work at .They could be working either individually and have their own office or they could have an office at a law firm.

Traits & Skills

  • Communication skills
  • Patience
  • Compassion
  • Research skills
  • Good knowledge of the law
  • Being a good problem solver
  • Being creative
  • Public speaking skills

Education/Degrees

  • Criminal Justice
  • International Relations
  • Economics/Finance
  • History
  • Political Science
  • Psychology
