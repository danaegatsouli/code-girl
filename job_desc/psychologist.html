Psychologist


Job Description

A phychologist is a perosn who studies the human mind, human emotions and behavior, and how different situations influence people. They use psychological evaluations and talk therapy to help people learn to better cope with life, relationship issues and mental health conditions.

Workplace

Psychologists work either in their own office, or they have an office in a place related to the specific field of psychology they have a degree at.For instance, school psycologists have their offices inside the school.

Traits & Skills

  • Analytical skills
  • Communication skills
  • Compassion
  • Being open minded
  • Patience
  • Being a good problem solver
  • Integrity
  • Observational skills

Education/Degrees
